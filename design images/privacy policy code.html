100% On-Device Processing

We prioritize security. All facial recognition and voice inputs are processed strictly locally inside your web browser using native web APIs. Biometric data or raw audio recordings are never sent, stored, or uploaded to any cloud servers. This "Local First" approach ensures that sensitive data never leaves your device.

Data Minimization & Trust

Your child's daily mood history is kept private and stored locally. No corporate tracking scripts or third-party ads exist in this ecosystem. You can permanently wipe all logs at any time by clearing your browser data or using the "Reset Workspace" button in your parent settings panel.
